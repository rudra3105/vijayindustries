Our Company

Vijay Industries, Vadodara

Established in 1991, Vijay Industries is a distinguished manufacturer and trader based at Makarpura G.I.D.C., Vadodara, Gujarat. We specialise in Rotating Air Rings, Plant Air Rings, Mono & Multilayer Film Plant Air Rings, Plastic Processing Machinery, Blown Film Plant Fabrication, Machinery Spares, Liner Bags, and Air Ring LD Tarpaulin Plant components.

Backed by experienced professionals and a modern infrastructure facility, we develop products that meet international quality standards — fully customised to client specifications.
