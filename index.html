About Vijay Industries

Distinguished Manufacturer Since 1991

Established in 1991, Vijay Industries is a distinguished manufacturer and trader of Rotating Air Rings, Plant Air Rings, Mono & Multilayer Film Plant Air Rings, Plastic Processing Plant and Machinery, Blown Film Plant Fabricator, Water Bath PP Tubing Plant, Machinery Spares and Parts, Air Ring Parts, Liner Bags, and Air Ring LD Tarpaulin Plant.

We are backed by a team of dedicated and experienced professionals working to international standards. Our facility at Makarpura G.I.D.C., Vadodara, is equipped with advanced machinery — allowing us to deliver fully customized products as per client specifications.
